We value your input and are here to help with any issues you may encounter. Here’s how you can report issues, provide feedback, and get support.

### Reporting Issues

If you encounter any problems while using our Minecraft AI Bots, please follow these steps to report them:

1. **Gather Information**: Collect as much information as possible about the issue, including any error messages and the steps to reproduce the problem.
2. **Take Screenshots**: Screenshots can be very helpful in diagnosing issues. Capture any relevant screenshots that show the problem.
3. **Post in Discord Forum**: Use our Discord forum to report the issue with the details you have gathered. You can do this by visiting our Discord forum.

### Providing Feedback

Your feedback is crucial in helping us improve our service. Here’s how you can share your thoughts and suggestions:

**Discord Channel**: Join our Discord server and post your feedback in the designated channels. Our community managers and developers actively monitor these channels.
    - [Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/elefant)

### Getting Support

If you need assistance with using our bots or have any questions, you can get support through the following channels:

**Discord Support**: Our support team is available on Discord to help you with any queries. Join our Discord server and post your questions in the support channel.
    - [Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/elefant)

For quick answers to common questions, check out our FAQ section. You might find the solution to your issue there.
